Bees and Trees

Cherry Cyser

Mead, Cider and Cherries
10 gallon recipe

15# Clover Honey
5gal Apple Cider
32oz Montmorency Tart Cherry Concentrate
Lalvin EC1116 Yeast
Safale S04 Yeast

Add honey (mead) to carboy, top up with spring water to 5.25 gallons.

Ferment the mead and cider separately in 5 gallon batches.

Mead uses EC1116 yeast, cider uses S04.

Once fermentation is complete and both have cleared, sulphate to end yeast activity and blend 50/50, add cherry concentrate.
