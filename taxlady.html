Dia the Tax Lady

West Coast IPA

A nod to Surly Brewing's
'Todd the Axeman'
5 gallon recipe

Grains
12# Golden Promise
2.5# Corn Sugar (Dextrose)
Hop Schedule
1.5oz Warrior 60 Minutes
1oz Citra 10 Minutes
.5oz Mosaic 10 Minutes
1oz Citra Whirlpool
.5oz Mosaic Whirlpool
White Labs WLP007 Yeast

Prepare 2 vials of WLP007 in a 2 liter starter a week before. Mash at 152F. After fermentation begin dry hopping. 2oz Citra and 1oz Mosaic for 7 days, then another 2oz Citra and 1 oz Mosaic for another 10 days.
