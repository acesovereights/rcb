Owenbrau

Russian Imperial Stout
aged on oak and whiskey

Our annual flagship brew in honor of our son Owen's birthday.
5 gallon recipe

Grains
12.75# Maris Otter
3.5# Munich Malt
1.5# Crystal 60L
1.5# Chocolate Malt
1.5# Special B Malt
1.5# Roasted Barley
1# Crystal 120L
1# Flaked Oats
3.5# Brown Sugar
Hop Schedule
1.12oz Columbus 80 Minutes
1.12oz Columbus 25 Minutes
1.12oz Columbus 10 Minutes
White Labs WLP007 Yeast

Prepare 2 vials of WLP007 in a 2 liter starter a week before. Soak medium toast oak spiral in 2oz George Dickel Whiskey for a week or longer. Mash in at 158F for 60 minutes. Whiskey and oak into secondary until desired taste is reached.
