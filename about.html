Luke Smith - Owner / Head Brewer

A home brewer for over 15 years. Started brewing extract only batches on a stovetop with a three gallon pot and a food-grade plastic bucket for a fermenter. That went well enough and soon the urge to make better beer took over. The next step was extract batches with some steeping grains, and then on to partial mash recipes. Soon the stove was out grown, an eight gallon turkey deep frying pot and propane burner took its place. Dozens of beers came from that simple one pot setup and a pair of glass carboys.

Gradually adding new equipment and fermenters led to the inevitable. The jump into all-grain brewing. A single tier stand was built from scratch with three 15-gallon vessels each fired by their own natural gas burner. A corner of the garage was taken over where the brewery now exists.
Finally. Year round brewing has come to Red Club Brewing.
